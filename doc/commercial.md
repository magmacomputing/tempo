# Commercial Support & Consulting

**Tempo** is a premium, high-performance library designed for reliability and ease of use. While the core library is open-source (MIT), we offer specialized support and consulting services for teams and enterprises with complex date-time requirements.

## 🚀 Why Commercial Support?

Working with time at scale—especially involving multiple timezones, calendars, and business-specific logic—can be one of the most bug-prone areas of any application. Commercial support ensures that your implementation is robust, efficient, and future-proof.

## 🛠️ Services Offered

### 1. Priority Support
Get direct access to the lead maintainer for bug reports and technical questions.
- **Guaranteed Response Times**: Skip the queue for critical issues.
- **Direct Communication**: Dedicated channel for your team's queries.

### 2. Architecture & Implementation Review
Building a scheduling engine, a financial reporting tool, or a complex calendar? We can review your architecture to ensure:
- Optimal use of `Temporal` and `Tempo`.
- Correct handling of timezone transitions and DST.
- Scalable performance for date-heavy calculations.

### 3. Custom Plugin (Term) Development
If your business operates on non-standard cycles (e.g., custom fiscal years, specialized academic calendars, or industry-specific date ranges), we can build and maintain custom **Term** plugins tailored to your exact needs.

### 4. Enterprise Integration
Help with migrating legacy date logic (e.g., from `Date` or `Moment.js`) to `Tempo` and `Temporal` with zero downtime and full test coverage.
